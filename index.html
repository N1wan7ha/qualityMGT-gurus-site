Total Quality Management (TQM)

The philosophy of Total Quality Management (TQM) is based on the notion that customer satisfaction is the key to an organization's long-term success. It highlights that quality is a comprehensive approach that encompasses all organizational functions and personnel, rather than just the lack of flaws in a good or service.

According to this fundamental perspective, quality is an ongoing endeavor that necessitates a dedication to process improvement at every level, from initial design and production to customer support and service. The broad definition of quality and the emergence of shared responsibility are essential to this description.
